BOOKING US

How to Book Frank "K" and Company



Frank "K" and Company varies in size from one to six musicians.

Fran, our drummer, is also an excellent vocalist who entertains as part of our Duo and Trio as well as all of the larger groups.

If you are looking for a big band sound, we can add brass and/or woodwinds along with our rhythm section.

We have a talented guitarist who sings and brings an added feature to the band.

Violins can be added for the cocktail and dinner hour.

Many events have smaller parties and the Duo or Trio can make your event something special to remember. Our musical talent, versatility and experience enable Frank "K" and Company to cater to your specific needs.

Needless to say, we can provide a variety of ensembles. We travel throughout Chicago, suburbs, and neighboring states. For additional information, please do not hesitate to contact Frank "K" via telephone (847 967 1184), email (Frank's Email), or by clicking here.
