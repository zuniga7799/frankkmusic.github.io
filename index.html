Book Us!


Frank "K" and Company varies in size from one to six musicians. Fran, our drummer, is also an excellent vocalist who entertains as part of our Duo and Trio as well as all of the larger groups. If you are looking for a big band sound, we can add brass and/or woodwinds along with our rhythm section. We have a talented guitarist who sings and brings an added feature to the band. Violins can be added for the cocktail and dinner hour.

About Us


Frank began his music career in his home town of Wisconsin playing with a three-piece band. He moved to Chicago early in his career to continue studying and performing with various groups. He formed his own band while continuing to play with many Chicago entertainers. While accordion was his childhood instrument, he also plays both piano and synthesizer.
